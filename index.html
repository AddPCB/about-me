This is my about me page.

My name is Peter Brightmore,
I am an experienced hospitality professional and an aspiring software developer.
Throughout my hospitality career, spanning over a decade, I have demonstrated a knack for problem-solving and meeting targets without oversight. This permitted my rise to Head Chef, despite lacking the relevant formal training or education.

In my role as head chef, I successfully led a small team in turning around a struggling kitchen, and I was instrumental in implementing standards and protocols that resulted in a flawless 5* hygiene accreditation.
I also managed due diligence, staff rotas, menu writing, and other office-based tasks.
My perfect attendance and punctuality made me a dependable asset.

Since the start of the pandemic, I have been looking to capitalize on my ADHD, rather than hide it. To that end, I am currently transitioning into software development, and I am in the second week of a full stack JavaScript boot camp. My goals are to build a strong foundational knowledge of the tools, operators and logic that form the basis of success, then become self-reliant in the industry by Q2 2023. Through this, I hope to truly make use of my resourcefulness, creativity and drive, which have been integral to my success in the hospitality industry.
